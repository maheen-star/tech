EndoTracker is a secure online portal providing endoscope management solutions for Customers and Partners of FiberTech Medical. EndoTracker provides instant access to all your maintenance records and a feature rich dashboard that monitors key metrics on equipment performance and maintenance.

With EndoTracker, you can quickly view and run reports that compile, list and analyze data about your equipment across multiple departments and facilities. Monitor the life cycle of your endoscope inventory as EndoTracker automatically captures and allocates service expenses, usage, and PM schedules.

Comprehensive Features

Endo Tracker will auto-update cumulative service expenditures and usage counts for each inventory item. (available when FiberTech provides service)

Quickly view the last repair event or complete service history for each inventory item. Set custom Spend Limits with notifications. Schedule and track preventive maintenance activities with notifications.
